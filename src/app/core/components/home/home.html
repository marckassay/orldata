How does this app have access to City of Orlando records?

In 2016, the City of Orlando, Florida took the initiative to increase public transparency by launching a website of theirs to "provide the public with immediate access to valuable City-specific data."

"The public publishing of data will also help the City to drive internal efficiencies at the City, develop opportunities for economic development, enhance community engagement, increase community trust and fuel Orlandoâ€™s civic tech ecosystem."

And with the City using Socrata services...

An Open Data API is available for public use! With this API software developers can query City of Orlando's records for: Building Permits Issued, Business Tax Receipts Issued, Orlando Police Department Cases and Calls for Service and Orlando Police Department Response to Resistance
